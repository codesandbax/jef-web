Jef Cunningham is a designer.

He loves simple, poetic expressions. His work has included apps, physical interfaces, retail designs, campaigns, websites, films, and more. He's a talented brainstormer and ideator, but also has a proven track record of collaborating with engineering teams to carry products to market. Jef has honed his skills at places like IDEO and Apple and his work has won several awards.

When he's not designing, he is dabbling in electronic music making, initiating short film projects, or going out for a morning surfing session. He also has his own entrepreneurial venture underway as the founder of a video making tool called Snowball.
