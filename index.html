Jef Cunningham is a designer who loves simplicity and poetry.

Jef grew up in the west of Ireland and studied both computer science and film en route to discovering interaction design. He has since practiced his craft at places like Apple and IDEO, where he discovered the magic of working in interdisciplinary teams. He's passionate about the whole product development journey, from concepting, to building, to iterating. He's currently building a video making tool called Snowball.

When he's not designing, Jef makes electronic music, experiments with personal projects, and gets in his morning surf.
